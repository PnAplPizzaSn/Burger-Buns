"Burger Buns"

Tagnzhong:

  • 2 T (20g) bread flour
  • 2 T (27g) water
  • 4 T (60g) whole milk

    • Dough:

  • 1/2 cup (120g) whole milk @95°F
  • 1 T (9g) instant yeast
  • 2.5 cups (320g) bread flour
  • 1 t (7g) fine sea salt
  • 2.5 T (35g) granulated sugar
  • 1 whole egg (room temp)
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 3 tablespoons (42g) unsalted butter, softened

    • Egg wash:

  • 1 whole egg
  • splash of whole milk

Instructions

    1. Combine milk and yeast, set aside
    2. Add milk and yeast mixture to dry ingredients with dough hook
    3. Add tagnzhong
    4. Add egg
    5. Add butter
    6. Knead for 5-8 minutes
    7. Shape dough and place it in a greased bowl
    8. Let rise until doubled
    9. Deflate dough
    10. Divide dough into 6
    11. Bring in corners of dough and shape into a ball
    12. Let rise until doubled
    13. Apply egg wash
    14. Bake at 375°F for 16-18 minutes
    15. Brush with butter
